## Overview

World Cup 2026 Simulator is a free football prediction tool for exploring group tables, score predictions, best third-place qualification, team paths, and knockout brackets. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website and how that information is used.

We designed the site so that the core simulator can be used without creating an account and without submitting personal predictions to us.

## Information We Collect

We may collect limited technical and usage information, including:

- Pages visited, language path, referring page, and broad interaction events
- Device and browser information such as browser type, operating system, screen size, and approximate region
- IP address and request metadata processed by our hosting and security providers
- Information you voluntarily send through a contact form or email

The simulator inputs you enter in the browser, such as match scores or group predictions, are primarily used to update the tool experience on your device.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Keep the website available, secure, and fast
- Understand which tools and pages are useful to visitors
- Improve rankings, score prediction flows, localization, and page quality
- Detect abuse, spam, scraping, or service reliability issues
- Respond to messages you send to us

## Analytics

We may use analytics services, including Google Analytics, to understand traffic patterns and improve the site. Analytics tools may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to estimate visits, sessions, page performance, and user interactions.

Analytics data is used in aggregate. We do not use analytics to identify individual visitors.

## Advertising And Third-Party Technologies

We may display advertising in the future, including ads served by Google or other advertising partners. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to:

- Deliver and measure ads
- Limit repeated ad views
- Detect invalid traffic and fraud
- Show personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your location, settings, and consent choices

Google's advertising services may use information from your visits to this and other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can manage Google ad personalization at:

https://adssettings.google.com/

## Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies may be used for essential site operation, language preferences, analytics, security, and advertising if advertising is enabled. For more detail, see our [Cookie Policy](/cookie).

## Legal Bases And Consent

Where required by law, including in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we will request consent before using non-essential cookies or personalized advertising technologies. You may be able to manage or withdraw consent through the cookie or privacy controls shown on the site.

## Service Providers

We may rely on service providers for hosting, security, analytics, email delivery, and website operations. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services.

The website is hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure. Cloudflare may process request metadata for security, caching, and delivery.

## Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, or operational reasons. Aggregated analytics may be retained for trend analysis.

## Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience interested in football tournament simulation. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. You can also use browser settings to block or delete cookies.

## Important Disclaimer

World Cup 2026 Simulator is an independent fan and analysis tool. It is not affiliated with FIFA, the World Cup, any national team, or any football federation. Predictions are for entertainment and informational purposes only.
